Pipes the New Filters in Angular :|

Squared Number is {{val | square}}


Sending Values to Pipes adding two to the resultant square {{val | square:2}}
Squared Number plus 2 is {{val | square:2}}

Passing Multiple Arguments is also possible in Pipe through array like
Squared Number plus 2 is {{val | square:[2,2]}} transform(value: number, args?: number[]): number


OR


Squared Number plus 2 is {{val | square:2,2}} transform(value: number, args?: number,args2?:number[]): number
